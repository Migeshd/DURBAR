PISCO BAR

Drawing inspiration from the oldest bars in Lima, the Pisco Bar provides a zestful setting for any occasion. Our expert mixologists are always on hand to shake up something from the vast selection of COYA signatures, Peruvian classics and localised specials. Guests can also enjoy the unique sounds of COYA music resident DJs every evening along with special weekly events with guest DJs.
